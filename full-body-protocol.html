Progression
& Tracking

How to Add Weight

  • Hit all sets & reps with clean form for 2 sessions in a row → add load.
  • Compound lifts (squat, deadlift, bench): +2.5–5 kg.
  • Isolation / DB lifts (curls, raises, kickbacks): +1–2.5 kg.
  • If you fail a set, repeat the same weight next session.

How to Track

  • Use the Log tab after every workout — sets & weight.
  • Check the Stats tab weekly to see your trend & PRs.
  • Update Body tab every 2 weeks (weight + photo).
  • Strength up + measurements stable = lean gain. ✓

Beginner Adjustments

  • Drop heavy compounds 1 set (e.g. 3×6 instead of 4×6).
  • Use machine versions before barbell versions when learning.
  • Skip finisher Day 4 first 4 weeks.
  • Focus on form & tempo over weight on the bar.

Recovery Rules

  • Sleep 7–9 hours — non-negotiable for growth.
  • 1.6–2.2 g protein per kg bodyweight, daily.
  • 1 full rest day between Day 2 (legs) & Day 4.
  • Deload week (50% volume) every 6–8 weeks.
